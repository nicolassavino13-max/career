# target_companies.md

## Purpose

**Agents:** company universe for research (Prompt §3). **Not** live pipeline — use `Career/applications_tracker.md`.

This file defines the company universe I should prioritize in my job search.

The goal is to focus on companies that improve my path toward a globally transferable fintech/payments/crypto-infrastructure career, with a medium-term target of USD 10k+ net monthly income and geographic flexibility.

This is not a generic dream-company list. It is a strategic target universe.

---

# 1. Company selection logic

Companies should be prioritized if they offer at least several of the following:

- Strong fintech, payments, crypto infrastructure or digital platform relevance
- Globally recognized brand
- Remote, Dubai, Europe or international optionality
- Roles in Strategy, BizOps, Corp Dev, Strategic Finance, GTM Strategy, Revenue Strategy, Product Strategy, Partnerships, Chief of Staff or GM-track
- High compensation potential
- Ability to build operator credibility beyond consulting
- Exposure to revenue, product, partnerships, ecosystem or business ownership
- Strong platform for future USD/global compensation

Avoid companies that are:
- Too local
- Too traditional
- Too bureaucratic
- Low compensation
- Weak brand
- Not transferable globally
- Too far from fintech/payments/technology platforms
