# role_scorecard.md

## Purpose

This file defines how I should evaluate job opportunities.

The goal is not to find “any good job.” The goal is to identify roles that move me from McKinsey Financial Services consulting into a globally transferable fintech/payments/crypto-infrastructure operator career, with a path toward USD 10k+ net monthly income and geographic flexibility.

Every opportunity should be assessed against this scorecard before I invest serious time in it.

---

# 1. Scoring system

Score each role from 1 to 5 on each dimension.

- 5 = excellent fit / strongly advances my goal
- 4 = strong fit
- 3 = acceptable / neutral
- 2 = weak fit
- 1 = poor fit / likely distraction

Some dimensions matter more than others. Use the weighted score to compare opportunities.

---

# 2. Core scoring dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | What it measures |
|---|---:|---|
| Fintech / payments / crypto relevance | 20% | Whether the role puts me closer to financial technology, payments infrastructure, digital banking, stablecoins, crypto infra or financial platforms |
| Global optionality | 20% | Whether the company/role improves my ability to work abroad, relocate, or get remote global roles later |
| Role fit | 15% | Whether the work matches Strategy, BizOps, Corp Dev, GTM Strategy, Revenue Strategy, Product Strategy, Strategic Partnerships, Chief of Staff or GM-track |
| Brand quality | 15% | Whether the company is respected globally or regionally enough to improve my CV |
| Compensation potential | 15% | Whether the role can realistically support my floor now and USD 10k+ net/month target later |
| Operator exposure | 10% | Whether I will move beyond consulting into execution, ownership, revenue, cross-functional work or business building |
| Lifestyle sustainability | 5% | Whether the workload is intense but sustainable, ideally not recreating MBB-style burnout |

Total = 100%

---

# 3. Detailed scoring guide

## A. Fintech / payments / crypto relevance — 20%

Score 5 if:
- Company is a global or regional fintech/payments/crypto infrastructure player
- Role is directly connected to payments, wallets, digital banking, stablecoins, financial infrastructure, embedded finance, acquiring, issuing, cross-border payments, or fintech platforms
- Experience would make me more credible for Adyen, Stripe, Revolut, Checkout.com, Visa, Mastercard, Coinbase, Kraken, Circle, Tether, Fireblocks, etc.

Score 4 if:
- Company is a digital platform with strong financial services adjacency
- Role is not pure fintech but connects to monetization, growth, platform economics, payments, marketplace, or financial services

Score 3 if:
- Company is strong but fintech relevance is indirect
- Role builds strategy/operator skills but not specific industry depth

Score 2 if:
- Company is mostly traditional finance or local business with limited fintech relevance

Score 1 if:
- Role pulls me away from fintech/payments/financial infrastructure

Examples:
- Revolut Strategy & Ops Revenue: 5
- Adyen Strategic Alliances: 5
- Mercado Livre Strategy & Corp Dev Ads/Marketing: 4
- Traditional bank strategy role: 2-3
- Generic corporate strategy in industrial company: 1-2

---

Score 5 if:
- Company is globally known
- Role can lead to remote global, Dubai, Europe, US/global fintech, or cross-border opportunities
- Company has international mobility, remote culture, or global teams
- Experience is understandable and valued outside Brazil

Score 4 if:
- Company is regionally dominant and respected internationally
- Role gives strong platform/business experience, even if less global

Score 3 if:
- Company is good locally but international transferability is uncertain

Score 2 if:
- Company is mostly Brazil-specific
- Role would be hard to explain globally

Score 1 if:
- Role locks me into a local Brazilian career path

Examples:
- Revolut: 5
- Adyen: 5
- Mercado Livre: 4
- Nubank: 4
- Mercado Pago: 4
- Local Brazilian bank: 2-3
- Traditional local corporate: 1-2

## C. Role fit — 15%

Score 5 if the role is one of:
- Strategy & Operations
- BizOps
- Corporate Development
- Strategic Finance with business ownership
- GTM Strategy
- Revenue Strategy
- Growth Strategy
- Product Strategy
- Strategic Partnerships / Ecosystem Strategy
- Chief of Staff
- GM-track role

Score 4 if:
- Role is commercial/partnerships-heavy but strategic, enterprise-level and cross-functional
- Role builds a missing muscle such as GTM, revenue or partnerships

Score 3 if:
- Role is adjacent but could become narrow
- Scope depends heavily on the team

Score 2 if:
- Role is too much account management, FP&A, project management or local operations

Score 1 if:
- Pure sales hunter
- Pure relationship manager
- Pure back-office
- Pure technical role
- Pure consulting loop

Examples:
- Revolut Strategy & Ops Revenue: 5
- Mercado Livre Strategy & Corp Dev: 5
- Adyen Senior Alliances Partner Manager: 4
- Pure enterprise sales: 2
- Traditional RM: 1-2

## E. Compensation potential — 15%

Score 5 if:
- Can realistically reach or exceed my target quickly
- Either pays USD/global compensation, Dubai-level net income, or BRL 30k+ net/month equivalent
- Has strong bonus/equity/upside
- Can lead to USD 10k+ net/month in 1-3 years

Score 4 if:
- May not hit target immediately but has strong trajectory and brand
- Total comp could approach target with bonus/equity

Score 3 if:
- Good compensation but likely below my floor
- Worth considering only if strategic value is exceptional

Score 2 if:
- Compensation likely materially below my floor

Score 1 if:
- Compensation would reset my market value downward

Current rough floor:
- For São Paulo: ideally BRL 30k+ net/month
- Below that only if brand/scope/upside is exceptional
- Long-term target: USD 10k+ net/month

## F. Operator exposure — 10%

Score 5 if:
- Role includes ownership of business outcomes
- I will work close to revenue, product, GTM, partnerships or execution
- I will be accountable for measurable impact
- Role helps me stop being perceived as only a consultant

Score 4 if:
- Role includes strategic work plus real implementation

Score 3 if:
- Role is analytical/strategic but still advisory-heavy

Score 2 if:
- Mostly decks, recommendations, internal coordination

Score 1 if:
- Pure advisory or support role with little ownership

Examples:
- Revolut S&O Revenue: 5
- Adyen Alliances: 5
- Mercado Livre Corp Dev: 4
- Internal strategy with little implementation: 3
- Consulting: 2-3

---

## G. Lifestyle sustainability — 5%

Score 5 if:
- Intense but sustainable
- Usually around 45-55 hours/week
- Flexibility or remote setup
- Travel/events manageable
- Leaves room for health, training, relationships and life

Score 4 if:
- Demanding but better than MBB
- Some spikes but manageable

Score 3 if:
- Unclear workload
- Could be intense but acceptable if upside is high

Score 2 if:
- Likely 60+ hours often
- High pressure and low control

Score 1 if:
- MBB-level or worse without equivalent upside

I can tolerate:
- occasional travel
- occasional evening events
- high-performance culture
- intense periods

I do not want:
- constant fire drills
- chronic weekend work
- low-control life
- permanent burnout

# 6. Red flags when scoring roles

Be careful if a role has any of the following:

- Local-only Brazilian business with little global relevance
- Traditional banking with limited fintech exposure
- Sales title but no strategic scope
- Account management disguised as partnerships
- Pure FP&A with no business ownership
- Strategy role with no execution or decision rights
- Weak brand and weak compensation
- Compensation below floor with unclear upside
- High hours but low learning/optionality
- Bureaucratic environment with low autonomy
- Role that makes me look less fintech-native than I do today

---

# 7. Final decision question

Before pursuing any opportunity seriously, ask:

> Does this role make me more likely to become a globally competitive fintech/payments/crypto-infrastructure operator earning USD 10k+ net/month with geographic flexibility?

If yes, pursue.

If no, avoid or treat as low priority.
