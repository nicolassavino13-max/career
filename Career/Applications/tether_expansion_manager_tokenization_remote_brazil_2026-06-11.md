# Original Job Description

Source: [Tether careers — Expansion Manager Tokenization](https://careers.tether.io/o/expansion-manager-tokenization-100-remote-6) · Remote · Expansion · Brazil / São Paulo

**Company context:** Tether — USDT stablecoin; **Hadron by Tether** = institutional platform for **real-world asset (RWA) tokenization**. Teams: Tether Finance, Power, Data, Education, Evolution. 100% remote global workforce.

**Role:** Expansion Manager on **Tokenization Expansion Team** — reports to **Head of Tokenization Expansion**. Support execution/delivery of client and partner initiatives across regions. Intersection of digital assets, tokenization, institutional finance.

**Responsibilities:**

- Support Head on active client/partner projects (onboarding → delivery)
- **Act as Account Manager** for assigned clients — communication, expectations, follow-up
- Coordinate clients ↔ product ↔ tech
- Manage ecosystem partner relationships
- Research, memos, insights for business/product decisions
- Track milestones, reporting, timely delivery
- Draft proposals, commercial documents, presentations
- Internal workflow / process improvements
- Support Hadron platform functional development per client/market needs

**Requirements:**

- 3–5 years in strategy consulting, investment banking, corporate finance, or business development
- Understanding of financial assets, capital markets, institutional finance
- Strong academic background (economics, finance, engineering)
- Analytical, communication, problem-solving
- Multiple projects; detail orientation; autonomous in high-stakes environments
- Blockchain/fintech/digital assets interest a plus
- **International travel** when required

**Application requirements (official form):**

- Why Tether; complex client project E2E; organizing multiple high-stakes projects; translating client needs to internal teams; RWA tokenization interest + 2–3 yr view; country of work; **expected annual salary (USD)**; LinkedIn
- **Policy: do not use AI tools on application answers** — disqualification risk

**Fraud notice:** Apply only via official channels (tether.recruitee.com / careers.tether.io); emails @tether.to or @tether.io only.
