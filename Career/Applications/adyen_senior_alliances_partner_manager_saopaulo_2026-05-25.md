- Work with Marketing teams on GTM activities with a commercial mindset to deliver strong ROI.
- Be an ambassador for Adyen's newly launched Partner Program.

Who you are:
- At least 5 years of working experience in a commercial / business development role.
- Born networker with high energy, self-starter, hands-on mentality, commercial edge, entrepreneurial mindset.
- In-depth knowledge of the local market.
- Proven commercial edge, track record of delivering results, ability to build long-term relationships.
- Storyteller with excellent presentation skills.
- Ability to balance strategic thinking with world-class execution.
- Excited by being responsible for commercial goals and creating measurable business impact.
- Willing to travel domestically and internationally, sometimes outside typical working hours.
- Full professional proficiency in English and Portuguese. Spanish is a plus.
- Available to work 3 days on site in Morumbi/SP.
