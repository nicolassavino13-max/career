# Original Job Description

Source: [Amazon.jobs — Job 10404183](https://www.amazon.jobs/en/jobs/10404183/sr-program-manager-payment-business-operation-latam) · Project/Program/Product Management--Non-Tech · BRA, SP, São Paulo

**Summary:** Sr Program Manager on **Amazon Latin America Payments Team** — lead partnership initiatives with banking partners, FIs, and payment service providers. Improve payment experience for Amazon customers in LATAM via partnerships with schemes, acquirers, and FIs. Expertise in **Brazilian payment methods (Pix, Boletos)** and credit card offerings.

**Responsibilities:**

- Develop and execute partnership strategies with banks, FIs, schemes, PSPs — focus Pix, Boletos
- Secure, sign-up, manage relationships with acquirers, issuers, schemes (Visa, Mastercard), APMs, 3PPs
- Negotiate and finalize business contracts with banking partners
- Drive SLAs through partners; data-backed service quality improvements
- Collaborate with Product, Tech, Legal, Finance — align partnerships with business objectives; payment method integration

**Day in the life:** Manage workload from payment partners; consolidate projects; internal advocate for partners; data-driven metrics; balance established partnerships + new payment solutions.

**Team:** LATAM Payment Business Operations — payment programs across LATAM (Brazil, Mexico stores; cross-border in 8 countries; Digital Businesses). Roadmaps for partner performance, contracts, system availability. Integration and Launches framework for non-card products and local payment methods.

**Basic qualifications:** Data/metrics for improvements; owning program strategy end-to-end + senior leadership communication; program/project management; English fluent.

**Preferred:** Master's/MBA quantitative field; process improvement leadership.
