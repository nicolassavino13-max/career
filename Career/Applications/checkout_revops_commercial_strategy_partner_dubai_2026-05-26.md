**Job description (from listing):**

Checkout.com is seeking a collaborative contributor to join the **Commercial Strategy & Performance** function within the **Revenue Operations** team. This contributor will serve as the key business partner to **MENA Commercial Leadership** (expanding across UAE and KSA), offering data-driven insights on business trends and commercial KPIs related to productivity. Responsible for the region's **revenue forecasting**, driven by a regular forecasting cadence and pipeline management process.

Agent for change on company-wide GTM initiatives: sales territory planning, verticalization, customer segmentation. Operationalize MENA Commercial strategy via structure, process improvements, and cross-functional projects (Product, Marketing, etc.).

**What you'll be doing:**
- Go-to-market planning
- Pipeline management and owning Commercial forecasting
- Sales territory planning, verticalization and segmentation
- Commercial reporting & insights to drive business discussions
- Contribute to shaping the company's commercial strategy
- Present to various stakeholders to influence data-driven actions
- Customer journey mapping & sales process improvement

**About you:**
- Previous experience in Management Consulting, Revenue/Business Operations, Strategy, Finance, or related
- Advanced Excel, CRM management, analytical mindset
- Strong communication; strategic, data-driven recommendations with leaders
- Comfortable with executives and cross-functional projects
- Fast-paced environment
- **Advantageous:** sales forecasting, territory planning, customer journey mapping, sales process; Looker and SQL

**Work model:** Hybrid — three days per week in office (per listing).
